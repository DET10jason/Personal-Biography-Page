About Me

Welcome everyone! My name is Jason Stanley. I'm 39 years old and from the greater Tulsa, Ok area. I have an amazing wife and three awesome kids. I am a graduate of Oklahoma State University with a Bachelors in Science. I also served just about 10 years in the U.S, Navy as an Intelligence Specialist to include three combat deployments to both Iraq and Afghanistan. Below are a list of my hobbies and likes:

  • College football, go Pokes!
  • Marvel movies and the comics
  • Camping
  • Traveling with my family
  • Golfing
  • Learning new things

My professional background stretches far back to 2007 when I joined the U.S. Navy to get into the Intelligence sector. I have specialized in Human Intelligence and counter terrorism for the better part of a decade. I then moved onto Geospatial Intelligence after I graduated college and becaome a GIS specialist, where I have worked professionally with privatized companies as well as local municipalities. Currently, I am looking to expand my career as a frontend web developer since completing Meta's frontend development certification program. Within this program, I have learned HTML and CSS as well as JavaScript functions. I have also gotten familiar with React/Node.js and UI/UX concepts.
